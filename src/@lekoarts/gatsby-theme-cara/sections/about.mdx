## About

> An easy snack box that hits your cravings without the guilt.

Dreamed up in a coffee shop in Seattle, Munchables are the stoner's go-to snack packs! Have you ever had the craving for every snack in your cabinet but didn't want to stuff yourself full? We sell Munchables so that you can get a taste of your favorites without breaking the bank or cleaning out your kitchen. Join us on a journey to beat the munchies!
